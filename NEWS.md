# gghinton 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `geom_hinton()` and `stat_hinton()` for drawing Hinton diagrams in ggplot2.
* `scale_fill_hinton()` for the conventional signed-data colour scheme.
* `theme_hinton()` for a clean, grid-free panel theme.
* `matrix_to_hinton()` and `as_hinton_df()` for converting matrices to tidy data frames.
